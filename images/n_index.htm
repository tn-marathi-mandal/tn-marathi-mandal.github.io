Welcome to Nashville, home of the TENNESSEE MARATHI MANDAL (TNMM)!
Nashville is a beautiful city full of all kinds of attractions.
We have the Country Music Hall of Fame Building in Nashville.
We have the NFL Football team TITANS and the Ice Hockey team PREDATORS in Nashville.
Nashville has the Huge OPRYLAND Hotel that can accomodate three Big Indoor Conventions at the same time.
Next to the OPRYLAND Hotel we have the famous GRAND OLE OPRY house where you can enjoy the Country Music programs.
The CMA (Country Music Award) is organized in Nashville each year when you get to see all the Big Stars in the Country Music Industry.
